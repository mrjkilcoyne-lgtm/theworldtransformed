## Check Current Status

1. **Core Functionality**
   - [ ] Interview flow (6 questions)
   - [ ] Tier selection (Basic/Standard/Council)
   - [ ] Report generation
   - [ ] Follow-up mechanism (1 month, 1 year)

2. **Questions Implementation**
   Review if these are properly implemented:
   1. What's the problem? (Open)
   2. How is it affecting you? (Impact/cost)
   3. What have you tried? (Prior attempts)
   4. What constraints exist? (Non-negotiables)
   5. What does solved look like? (Ideal state)
   6. Are you serious about this? (Commitment check)

3. **Tier Pricing**
   - Basic: Free (single model)
   - Standard: 10p (enhanced analysis)
   - Council: 50p (multi-model synthesis)

4. **Privacy & Data**
   - Reports are private (only user sees them)
   - No data storage without consent
   - Client-side generation where possible

5. **Payment Integration**
   - Stripe for micro-transactions
   - Donation links (Ko-fi/similar)
