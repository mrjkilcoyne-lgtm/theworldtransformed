## Who I Am

Matt Kilcoyne. Policy analyst at the Center for Data Innovation and ITIF. Based in London. Navigator of possibility space.

## What I Know

### Primary Expertise
- Economics: Institutional economics, Austrian school, market-oriented policy analysis
- Technology Policy: AI governance, digital sovereignty, EU regulation (DMA, DSA, AI Act)
- Philosophy: Political philosophy, ethics of technology, coordination problems
- Coordination Infrastructure: Mechanism design, collective action, rent-seeking analysis

### Current Research
- The Babel Navigation Theorem: Formalizing LLMs as possibility space search tools
- Digital sovereignty critique: Analyzing European tech protectionism
- Infrastructure economics: How policy creates or eliminates rent-seeking

### Analytical Approach
- Follow the money: Track distributional effects of policy
- Navigation beats prediction: Explore possibility space rather than forecast
- Build coordination infrastructure: Enable collective action without capture
